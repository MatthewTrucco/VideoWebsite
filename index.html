Services

What We Restore & Digitize

We focus on the formats most families still have tucked away in closets and basements. If you’re not sure what you have, send us a photo and we’ll help identify it.

VHS Tapes

Standard VHS tapes from home camcorders or VCR recordings. We convert them to digital files and clean up color and brightness where possible.

VHS-C & MiniDV

Smaller camcorder tapes like VHS-C and MiniDV are carefully adapted and transferred with professional equipment for smooth playback.

Slides

35mm and similar slides scanned at high resolution. Perfect for archiving family trips, weddings, and special events.

Printed Photos

Loose prints and photo stacks scanned and organized into labeled folders so you can easily share and reprint your favorites.

How It Works

We keep the process simple and transparent. You always know where your memories are and what’s happening to them.

1

Drop Off

Bring your tapes, slides, and photos to us in Basking Ridge. No shipping for now

2

Inspect & Prepare

We inspect each item and, where possible, gently clean it to help get the best transfer quality.

3

Digitize & Restore

Your media is converted to digital files with basic color and exposure correction. We can trim obvious blank or static footage on request.

4

Deliver & Return

You receive your files via USB drive or secure download, and all original tapes and photos are returned to you.

About & FAQ

Local Care for Irreplaceable Memories

Matt's Video Restoration is a small, local service in Basking Ridge. We treat your tapes and photos like they’re our own family history.

How long does it take?

Most projects are finished in 1–2 weeks. Larger projects may take longer, but we’ll give you a clear estimate before we start.

Will you damage my tapes?

We do everything we can to avoid damage and only run tapes through professional-grade equipment. If we notice serious damage, we’ll contact you before proceeding.

What format are the digital files?

Video is typically delivered as MP4 files, and images as high-resolution JPEG or PNG. If you need something specific, just ask.

Do you offer DVDs or USB drives?

Yes. We can put your videos and photos on USB drives, or burn DVDs on request. Extra copies are available for an additional fee.
